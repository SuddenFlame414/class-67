Once upon a time, there lived a monkey, He stayed on an Island, filled with trees that gave delicious mangoes.
One day, a crocodile came to the shore looking for food. The monkey pitied him and gave him some mangoes.
Fast forward 10 months
The two animals are good friends now. One day, when the crocodile came home with more mangoes, his wife, the female crocodile, got angry and said,
"Why do you keep getting me mangoes?"
